About KR3 Performance

Built from real on-field experience and a passion for developing the next generation of ballplayers.

KR3 Performance was created to give young athletes an edge — not just in mechanics, but in how they think the game, prepare, and carry themselves as teammates and leaders.


As a former Division I player at Prairie View A&M, I know firsthand what it takes to compete at a high level. My goal is to take that knowledge, simplify it, and deliver it in a way that youth players can actually use.


Sessions are structured, intentional, and encouraging — players work, learn, and leave with clear takeaways they can apply in games and practices.

What parents can expect:

• Clear communication and honest feedback
• A structured plan for each athlete’s growth
• Focus on both fundamentals and confidence
• A coach who cares about the person, not just the stats

Training Services

Sessions are tailored by age, experience level, and goals — from first-timers to serious competitors.

Hitting

Private Hitting Sessions

Swing mechanics, approach, timing, and barrel control. Drills that build repeatable swings and in-game confidence at the plate.

Defense

Fielding & Footwork

Infield and outfield work focusing on footwork, glove presentation, transitions, and throwing mechanics that translate on game day.

Remote

Video Breakdown

Send game or practice clips for detailed breakdowns, corrections, and drill recommendations. Perfect for remote players.

Mindset

Confidence & Game IQ

Teach players how to think the game, handle failure, and build routines that support long-term development.
